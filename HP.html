SWEET OVERNIGHT OATS

INGREDIENTS:
  • 1. ⅓ cup old-fashioned oats
  • 2. ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 3. 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 4. 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 5. ½ cup milk of choice
  • 6. ½ cup fruit
  • 7. Drizzle of maple syrup or honey
    • METHOD
  • 1. Combine the muesli, chia seeds and nut butter. Add a splash of the milk and mix the nut butter into the oats. Then add the rest of the milk and stir to combine.
  • 2. Top with your fruit of choice.
  • 3. Place the lid on the jar and refrigerate overnight, or up to 5 days. When you’re ready to serve, add a drizzle of maple syrup or honey if you’d like, and enjoy chilled.
